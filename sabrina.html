Chilling Adventures of Sabrina



What is it?

Chilling Adventures of Sabrina is an American supernatural horror streaming television series developed by Roberto Aguirre-Sacasa for Netflix, based on the Archie comic book series of the same name.



Plot

Chilling Adventures of Sabrina is set in the fictional town of Greendale. It is a dark coming-of-age story that includes horror, fear and witchcraft. Sabrina Spellman must reconcile her dual nature as a half-witch, half-mortal while fighting the evil forces that threaten her, her family, and the daylight world humans inhabit.







Review

The terrifying adventures of Sabrina are about Satanism and magic, but the series in many ways is also inspired by the characters of Shakespeare's tragedies.
